Professional Career Goals

My career aspirations are focused on applying my finance and accounting skills in meaningful healthcare and banking environments. I'm actively pursuing opportunities that align with my academic background in finance with an accounting minor, and my passion for process improvement and strategic planning. Through my involvement in BizDogs, Women in Business, and the Student Healthcare Management Association, I've developed strong leadership and collaborative skills that prepare me for professional success in customer-facing and operational finance roles.

I'm hoping to secure a finance internship with Essentia Health during my junior year. I'm also deeply interested in process improvement and strategic planning, which align with the kind of work Essentia's finance team engages in. With my technical background, emotional intelligence, and collaborative mindset, I'd be a great fit for their finance department if an opportunity becomes available.
